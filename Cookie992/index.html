Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Cat Crunch - Match 3 Games uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our game and website.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used by website owners to make their websites work, or to work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information.

Cookies set by Cat Crunch - Match 3 Games are called "first-party cookies". Cookies set by parties other than us are called "third-party cookies". Third-party cookies enable third-party features or functionality to be provided on or through the website (e.g., advertising, interactive content, and analytics). The parties that set these third-party cookies can recognize your device both when it visits our website and when it visits certain other websites.

Important: Cat Crunch - Match 3 Games respects your right to privacy and is committed to being transparent about the technologies it uses. This Cookie Policy forms part of our wider Privacy Policy.

Why Do We Use Cookies

We use first-party and third-party cookies for several reasons. Some cookies are required for technical reasons in order for our website and game to operate, and we refer to these as "essential" or "strictly necessary" cookies. Other cookies also enable us to track and target the interests of our users to enhance the gaming experience and provide personalized features. Third parties serve cookies through our website for advertising, analytics, and other purposes.

The specific types of first and third-party cookies served through our website and the purposes they perform are described below:

Types of Cookies We Use

The cookies we use can be categorized as follows:

Type of Cookie Purpose
Essential Cookies These cookies are strictly necessary to provide you with services available through our website and game, and to use some of its features, such as access to secure areas. Without these cookies, services you have asked for, like secure login or game progress saving, cannot be provided.
Performance & Analytics Cookies These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our site and game. They help us to know which pages are the most and least popular and see how visitors move around the site. All information these cookies collect is aggregated and therefore anonymous.
Functionality Cookies These cookies enable the website and game to provide enhanced functionality and personalization. They may be set by us or by third-party providers whose services we have added to our pages. If you do not allow these cookies, then some or all of these services may not function properly.
Targeting & Advertising Cookies These cookies may be set through our site by our advertising partners. They may be used by those companies to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant advertisements on other sites. They do not store directly personal information but are based on uniquely identifying your browser and internet device.

Game Progress: We use cookies to save your game progress, level achievements, and settings. These are essential for the proper functioning of Cat Crunch - Match 3 Games.

How to Control Cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can exercise your cookie preferences through our Cookie Consent tool that appears when you first visit our website.

You can also set or amend your web browser controls to accept or refuse cookies. If you choose to reject cookies, you may still use our website, but your access to some functionality and areas of our website may be restricted. As the means by which you can refuse cookies through your web browser controls vary from browser to browser, you should visit your browser's help menu for more information.

Cookies in Mobile Games

When you use our mobile game application, we and our third-party partners may use cookies and similar technologies to track your use of the game, target advertisements to you, and collect information about your device and game usage.

In mobile applications, cookies are not always used in the traditional sense. Instead, similar technologies like "device identifiers" and "mobile advertising identifiers" (such as Apple's IDFA or Google's Advertising ID) are used for similar purposes.

You can opt out of allowing mobile advertising identifiers to be used for targeted advertising:

  • On iOS: Go to Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Turn on "Limit Ad Tracking"
  • On Android: Go to Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
